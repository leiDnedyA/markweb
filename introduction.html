Markweb

About

Markweb (currently in beta) de-clutters the web for reading. When you load a page in Markweb, a bot will filter out the unnecessary parts (ads, headers, etc). Then, consistent and clean formatting will be applied, and you will be presented with a nice, more readable version of the original webpage, leaving the original content intact.
