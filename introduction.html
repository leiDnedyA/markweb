About Markweb

Markweb de-clutters the web for reading. To understand how links work, try clicking the '→' sign beside this link, and then click the link itself: How to Do Great Work (Paul Graham).

To open Markweb from a webpage (let's say you're coming from https://example.com), you can simply add leidnedya.github.io/markweb/#<your-url-here> before the url.
